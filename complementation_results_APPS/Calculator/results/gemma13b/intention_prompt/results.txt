GUI Intentional Tests & Action Sequences:

Here are some test cases designed to validate the core functionality, with increasing complexity:

Test 1: Basic Addition

Objective: Verify addition functionality.
Actions:
Click "7" button. (Verify '7' appears in the display)
Click "+" button (Not present in the provided data, assuming it exists).
Click "3" button. (Verify '3' appears in the display)
Click "=" button. (Verify result '10' appears in the display)
Test 2: Basic Multiplication

Objective: Verify multiplication functionality.
Actions:
Click "7" button.
Click "*" button.
Click "3" button.
Click "=" button. (Verify result '21' appears in the display)
Test 3: Mixed Operations (Order of Operations)

Objective: Verify correct order of operations.
Actions:
Click "7" button.
Click "*" button.
Click "3" button.
Click "+" button.
Click "2" button.
Click "=" button. (Verify result '23' appears in the display)
Test 4:  Using Less Than and Greater Than Operators

Objective: Verify functionality of less than/greater than operators.
Actions:
Click "7" button.
Click "<" button.
Click "8" button.
Click "=" button. (Verify result 'false' or equivalent appears in the display)
Click "7" button.
Click ">" button.
Click "6" button.
Click "=" button. (Verify result 'true' or equivalent appears in the display)
Test 5:  Zero Division Error Handling

Objective: Verify error handling when attempting to divide by zero.
Actions:
Click "7" button.
Click "/" button (Not present in provided data, assuming it exists).
Click "0" button.
Click "=" button. (Verify an appropriate error message appears - e.g., "Division by zero")
Test 6:  Multiple Operations and Clearing

Objective: Verify the ability to perform multiple operations and clear the display.
Actions:
Click "7" button.
Click "*" button.
Click "3" button.
Click "=" button. (Verify result '21' appears)
Click "<" button. (Verify the display clears or returns to 0).
Click "7" button.
Click "+" button.
Click "3" button.
Click "=" button. (Verify result '10' appears)
