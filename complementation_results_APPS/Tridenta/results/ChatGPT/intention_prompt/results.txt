3. GUI Intentional Test Scenarios

Here’s a structured set of tests to validate the screen’s main functionality.

A. Region Selection

Action: Tap region "1" on the map.
Expectation: Only data for region 1 should be highlighted/displayed.

Action: Tap region button "2" in the legend.
Expectation: Map highlights area 2; content updates to region 2.

Action: Tap multiple regions in sequence (e.g., "3", then "6").
Expectation: Last selected region becomes active; prior selection is deselected.

Action: Tap the same region again.
Expectation: Region selection toggles off (returns to default or "All").

B. City Selection

Action: Tap “Pergine”.
Expectation: Only information related to Pergine is shown.

Action: Tap “Rovereto”, then “Trento”.
Expectation: City filter updates accordingly; only one city is active at a time.

C. Transport Mode Filters

Action: Tap “Railway”.
Expectation: Only railway routes appear.

Action: Tap “Funicular”.
Expectation: Only funicular routes appear.

Action: Tap “All”.
Expectation: All transport modes are shown again.

D. Combined Filters

Action: Select Region 4 + City “Trento” + Transport “Railway”.
Expectation: Results filtered by region 4, city Trento, and railway only.

Action: Change transport filter from “Railway” to “Funicular” without changing region.
Expectation: View updates instantly, keeping region filter intact.

E. Reset & Navigation

Action: Open “Selected area” dropdown in top bar.
Expectation: User can reset or choose a global “All” option.

Action: Tap filter icon (top-right).
Expectation: Additional filtering/sorting options appear (or panel expands).
